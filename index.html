How do I receive digital products?

After completing your purchase, a download link is sent instantly to the email you provide at checkout. No account required.

Where are physical orders picked up?

All physical products (bracelets, brownies, bookmarks) are available for local pickup in Wilton Manors, FL. Address and scheduling details are sent to your email after purchase.

Are the brownies actually dairy-free?

Yes — every recipe is formulated without dairy and baked fresh to order. If you have specific allergen concerns, reach out before ordering.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Digital product sales are final once delivered. For physical products, contact us within 48 hours of pickup and we'll make it right.

Do you ship physical products?

Currently, physical products are local pickup only (South Florida). Shipping is in development — sign up for the newsletter to be notified first.

Can bundles mix digital and physical items?

Yes. The digital portion delivers to your inbox immediately. The physical items in bundles require local pickup — you'll receive details after purchase.
